Product

ClearanceGate

Visit ClearanceGate Official Site (External)

A Formal Authorization Layer Placed Immediately Before High-Risk Execution

ClearanceGate is not a prediction or decision engine. It is an authorization layer that explicitly determines whether an execution is allowed according to deterministic policy rules. Rather than replacing existing workflows, it forms a governance boundary directly before execution.

Outcomes are defined as PROCEED / BLOCK / REQUIRE_ACK / DEGRADE. Under uncertainty, it enforces fail-closed behavior and preserves structured audit trails.

  • Deterministic outcome
  • Fail-closed under uncertainty
  • Auditability by design

CI/CD Approval Gate

Controls production-release approvals and improves operational risk visibility.

Execution Control for High-Risk Operations

Installs a policy boundary before critical actions to suppress accidental execution.

Structured Audit Trails

Captures approver identity, rationale, and outcomes for accountability and compliance.
